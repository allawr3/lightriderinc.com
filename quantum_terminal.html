What is the Quantum Terminal?

The Quantum Terminal is a revolutionary device that harnesses the fundamental unpredictability of quantum physics to generate true random numbers. Unlike conventional pseudorandom number generators that use mathematical algorithms, the Quantum Terminal leverages quantum mechanics to produce entropy that is genuinely unpredictable and impossible to replicate.
